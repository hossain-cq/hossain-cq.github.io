    <h2>Research contributions</h2>
    <p>
      My research develops reproducible computational frameworks integrating
      first-principles physics, machine learning, and near-term quantum algorithms
      to address bottlenecks in materials discovery and chemical simulation.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="grid">

  <article class="card">
      <h3>Quantum algorithms for spectral problems</h3>
      <p>
        I develop and apply quantum subspace and spectral filtering techniques
        (VQE, qEOM, SQD, and filtered-QPE) to resolve low-lying eigenstates,
        excitation energies, and dissociation pathways in molecular and
        materials systems.
      </p>
